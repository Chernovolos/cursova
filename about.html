Title: Office

"The Office" (English: The Office US) is an American comedy series of the NBC channel. It is an adaptation of the British TV series of the same name on the BBC channel. The Office was adapted for American audiences by Greg Daniels, who previously worked for many years as a writer for Saturday Night Live, as well as King of the Hill and The Simpsons. The series is produced by Daniels' Deedle-Dee Productions, Shine America and Universal Television. The original executive producers were Daniels, Howard Klein, Ben Silverman, Ricky Gervais and Stephen Merchant, with several others joining the list later.

Memorial complex "Fighters for the freedom of Ukraine"

Opened on June 19, 2005 on the territory of the former prison, which operated since 1898 until 1963. The exposition of the complex is dedicated to the memory of prisoners and martyrs within these walls. The main components of the complex are, first of all, a symbolic wrought iron fence, which consists of elements that resemble the hands of martyrs outstretched to the sky. The fence is entwined with rose branches - the personification of the unruly, spiritual struggle the beauty of the dead who gave their lives for the will of Ukraine. From the sides of the entrance gate — fragments of a wall with bars on the windows, reminding passers-by of a place of imprisonment. Then the entrance arch with a crown of thorns at the top and a memorial stele. Behind the entrance arch — a two-story building of the museum, opposite which is a sculptural composition "Pieta" (a mother holds the body of her dead son in her arms). In the depth of the yard is a wall Sorrows in the form of a large black cross and a bas-relief of martyrs. Nearby is a sarcophagus grave. The observation tower of those times has also been preserved. Commemorative bells have been installed on the premises of the former prison.

Peter Obal Museum

From 1942 to 1987, the European-level artist Petro Obal lived in Stryi. The artist was born on April 19, 1900 in the village of Collar in Ternopil Oblast. Finished Kraków Academy of Fine Arts, worked as a drawing teacher in the schools of the time Poland, participated in art exhibitions. From 1950 to 1956, he — a prisoner of Stalin's camps in Kazakhstan. Returning from exile to Stryi, continued to work fruitfully. The artist died on May 26, 1987. Petro Obal is a famous landscape painter, portraitist and graphic artist. He created during his life more than 700 paintings.
